Types of Movie Genres

Action,Adventure and Horror are different types of Movie Genres

Movie genres are categories used to classify films based on their narrative elements, themes, and stylistic approaches. These classifications help audiences identify the type of cinematic experience they can expect. From heart-pounding action flicks to thought-provoking dramas, movie genres offer a diverse array of storytelling styles to suit various tastes and preferences.
