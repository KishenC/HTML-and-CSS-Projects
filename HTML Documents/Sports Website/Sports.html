Types of Various Sports

Football,Boxing,Golf and Darts are different types of Sport

There are 10 types of Sports, including Individual Sports are played by one person and do not involve any opposing players or teams. Examples include running,swimming and cycling. Individual sports are known to focus on personal achievement and self-improvement. Team Sports are played by two or more opposing teams, with the goal of scoring points and winning the game example include basketball, soccer and football. Team Sports are known for emphasis on teamwork, communication and strategy, Contact Sports are sports that involve physical contact between players such as tackling or blocking, examples include football, rugby and hockey. Contact Sports are known for their physicality and emphasis on Strength and Endurence, Non-Contact Sports are sports that do not involve physical contact between players such as golf or tennis, Outdoor Sports are played in outdoor environments such as parks or fields, Indoor Sports are played in indoor environments such as gyms or arenas. Examples include basketball, volleyball amd racquetball. Water sports are played in or on water, such as swimming, diving, and water polo Endurance Sports are sports that require sustained physical effort over a long period of time such as running, cycling and swimming Strength Sports that require physical strength and power such as Weightlifting and Powerlifting Combat Sports are sports that involve physical combat between players, such as boxing, wrestling and martial arts, this type of sports are known for technique, strategy and discipline.
